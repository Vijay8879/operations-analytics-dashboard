# 📘 Business Logic

## Forecast

Expected customer orders for the selected day.

---

## Booked Orders

Total customer orders booked.

---

## Delivered Orders

Orders successfully delivered.

---

## Achievement %

Achievement % = Delivered Orders / Forecast × 100

---

## Gap

Gap = Forecast − Delivered

A negative gap indicates the forecast has not yet been achieved.

---

## AOV (Average Order Value)

Average value of each customer order.

Formula:

Revenue / Orders

---

## UPO (Units Per Order)

Average units sold in each order.

Formula:

Units / Orders

---

## Product Deactivation

The dashboard categorizes inactive products based on business priority.

| Category | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| C0 | Top 50 ranked products currently deactivated |
| C1 | Products ranked 51–100 currently deactivated |
| C2 | Products ranked 101–200 currently deactivated |
| C3 | Products ranked 201+ currently deactivated |

The cumulative C0 trend helps monitor critical product availability throughout the day.
